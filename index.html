Team ZRescue is a group of Tech Enthusiasts, who are working towards developing a cross-platform device that can be integrated into any vehicle to rescue victims injured in road accidents. Automating the whole process of causality detection and sending information to the nearby hospitals and injured people’s family members by SMS and call, which eventually reduces the chance of losing a life. To summarise, we are helping to reduce the casualties due to accidents.
